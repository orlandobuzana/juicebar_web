The Juice Box Cleasenses

The Juice Box juice cleanse includes six, 16 ounce drinks per day made with fresh fruit and vegetable juice blends. No added sugar, no preservatives, no chemicals. Just juice. We offer 2 different cleanse options. Our Revive Cleanse is ideal for those who are new to juicing or cleansing and enjoy vivacious tasting beverages to aid them through their cleanse. Our Live Green Cleanse provides a deeper cleanse with only green juices, taking the cleanse a little bit further. Cleanses are available in 1, 3 & 5 day packages.
